Events are not opinions, overviews, or long pieces.

Pick a few good topics where the event should appear.

The title is a lede, not a headline.

  • Describe the event, its importance, and the context as concisely as possible.
  • Use the present tense and the active voice.
  • Usually, identify a person as the subject of your title.
  • Use sentence capitalization.
  • End the sentence with a period.
  • No clickbait.
  • Don't cite the news source in your title.
  • If the core idea is a quotation, use:
    Speaker Name: "Quotation goes here."
